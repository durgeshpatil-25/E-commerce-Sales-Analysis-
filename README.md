# Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## Overview
This dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Madhav E-Commerce sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, and product insights. It helps businesses track important KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Key Insights
- Total Sales Amount: **₹438K**
- Total Profit: **37K**
- Total Quantity Sold: **5.6K**
- Average Order Value (AOV): **121K**

### Business Highlights
- **Clothing** and **Electronics** are the most profitable categories.
- Highest profits were generated in **January** and **November**.
- **Maharashtra** is the top-performing state by profit.
- **Credit Card** and **COD** are the most preferred payment methods.
- **Printers** and **Bookcases** are among the highest profit-generating products.
